About Me

Hello! I'm Yannis Vosnakis, a passionate software developer with a strong background in computer science and a diverse range of professional experiences. I hold a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Derby, complemented by studies in Business at the University of Exeter.

With four years of programming experience, I have honed my skills in various programming languages including C, C#, C++, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Python. My expertise spans web development, data analytics, and effective communication, enabling me to excel in both individual and team settings.

My journey through different roles—ranging from English Tutor and Fleet Manager to Founder of a business—has equipped me with a unique skill set and a robust work ethic. I've developed efficient systems, provided top-notch customer service, and managed teams, all while maintaining a keen eye for detail and a commitment to excellence.

Fluent in English and Greek, with intermediate proficiency in Italian and basic knowledge of Spanish, I bring a multicultural perspective to my work. I am always eager to take on new challenges and contribute to innovative projects.

Web Scraping Currency Converter

I created a web scraping currency converter application in Python for my final dissertation project at university. I used BeautifulSoup and web scraping techniques to scrape data from x-rates.com. The program is visually appealing, incorporating flags next to the currencies, and can be accessed via desktop.
